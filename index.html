How to Order

Your brownie cravings, sorted in 4 simple steps!

Step 1

Select your desired portion from the menu.

Step 2

Fill in your delivery details on the order page.

Step 3

Choose payment method: Easypaisa, bank transfer, or Cash on Delivery.

Step 4

Confirm and place your order — we’ll take it from there!

Note: Since FudgeBar is a fresh and growing brand, each batch is prepared with love. Please place your order at least 1 day in advance to ensure timely delivery.
